## multicore testing
```r
library('parallel')
testFun <- function(x, size){
  set.seed(1)
  sum(rnorm(size))
}
cl <- makeCluster(detectCores())
system.time(clusterApply(cl, 1:1000, testFun, size=1e6))
stopCluster(cl)
```
